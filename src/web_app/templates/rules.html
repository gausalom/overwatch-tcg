Deck Construction

Each player must build a 40-card deck. Each deck must belong to a single faction:

  • Vanguard
  • Dominion
  • Omnic
  • Outlanders

A deck may include Overwatch faction cards, as they are neutral and usable in all factions.

A deck cannot contain Location cards.

In addition to the main deck, players must have a clearly differentiated Energy Orb deck consisting of 10 Energy Orbs.

You may include no more than 3 copies of any card in your deck.



Game Setup

  1. Decide which player goes first. It is recommended to roll a die. The winner chooses to go first or second.
  2. Randomly determine which map will be played. (In competitive play, this rule may vary.)
  3. Shuffle your deck.
  4. Each player draws 5 cards.
    • The Defender receives one temporary Energy Orb. Once used, it is discarded and cannot return to the game.
    • Each player can put all his hand on the bottom of the deck and draw 5 new cards and shuffle the deck.


Turn Structure


Start of Turn


  1. Ready all cards (Heroes and Energy Orbs).
  2. Add 2 ready Energy Orb to your reserve.
  3. Conquer.
    • For each Location, compare total Conquest values.
    • The winning player advances a number of points equal to the difference between both players.
    • If the Conquest value is greater than 0 (meaning at least one Hero on each side) and both players are tied, each player advances 1 point.
    • If the Conquest value surpasses 3, 6 and 8, the opponent can Scan +1. If the opponent conquers directly by passing through two points, Scan +2.
    • Once a team reaches the required Conquest points for a Location, it becomes a Conquered Location, and no further conquest checks are made for it. However, Heroes may still use that Location.
  4. Trigger all "At the start of turn" triggers.
  5. Draw a card.

Main Phase


You may perform the following actions in any order and repeatedly unless otherwise specified:

  • Play a card.
  • Move a Hero to a Location by spending 1 Energy Orb.
  • Activate a Hero’s ability by paying its associated cost.
    Each ability may only be used once per turn.
  • Exhaust a Hero to challenge another Hero.
    • When two Heroes attack each other, they deal damage simultaneously.
    • If a Hero has both temporary shields and regular shields, the controller chooses which to use first.
    • If a Hero’s health reaches 0 or less, it is defeated and placed in the discard pile.
    • A Hero can not attack the turn is played.
  • If you play a Hero and already control 5 Heroes, you must sacrifice one.
  • Use a Location ability. You may only do so if you have at least one Hero there. Each ability may only be used once per turn.

End of Turn


  • Trigger all "At the end of turn" triggers.


Victory Condition

A player wins when they control the majority of Locations:

  • On 3-Location maps: control 2.
  • On 5-Location maps: control 3.
  • On single-Location maps: control that Location.

At the end of the turn, during the conquest check, if a player controls the majority of Locations, the game immediately ends.
